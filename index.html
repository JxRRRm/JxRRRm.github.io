Summary

One Piece is an anime that is still current with over 1000 episodes. In the story, the main character, Monkey D. Luffy
has a mission to be the king of the pirates. But in order to accomplish this goal, Luffy first finds members to join his crew.
As the story progresses, Luffy successfully recruits several key members for his pirate crew, including a navigator, a cook,
as well as a doctor. Together Luffy and his crew are the Straw Hat Pirates and their main goal is to help Luffy find the
One Piece and become the king of the pirates.
